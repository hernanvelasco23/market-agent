# Historical Candle Engine

## Goal

Add historical candle support to improve the quality of MarketAgent signal generation.

The current signal engine relies mostly on intraday snapshots.
This feature will allow the platform to calculate real technical indicators using historical market data.

## Business Value

This improves:
- signal quality,
- trend detection,
- pullback validation,
- risk classification,
- and AI-generated market briefings.

It also enables:
- EMA-based analysis,
- RSI calculations,
- ATR-based stops,
- relative strength analysis,
- and better market regime detection.
